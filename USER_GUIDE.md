# 🏆 DG World Cup 2026 Score Predictor - User Guide

Welcome to the ultimate **DG Score Predictor**! This guide will help you understand how to navigate the platform, make predictions, understand the scoring system, and climb the live leaderboard to win amazing prizes.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Account Setup
- **Register / Join Free**: Click the **Join Free** button on the home page or go to `/register` to create a new profile.
- **Sign In**: Access your dashboard anytime by signing in at `/login`.
- **Profiles**: Ensure you verify and fill in your profile name.

### 2. The Predictor Board (Dashboard)
- Once signed in, you will be taken to the dashboard showing the active/upcoming matches.
- You can navigate through dates using the date selection tabs at the top of the matches list.

---

## 🔮 Making & Saving Predictions

### How to Predict:
1. Locate the match you want to predict on the daily fixture list.
2. Enter your predicted score for both the **Home Team** and the **Away Team** in the input boxes.
3. Click the **Save & Join Leaderboard** button at the bottom of the page to save your predictions.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Match Lock Time:** Predictions are locked exactly at kickoff time for each match. Once a match is locked, you cannot change your prediction, and the input fields will show as locked (🔒).

---

## 📊 How Points Work

Your predictions will score points depending on how close they are to the final match results published by the administration.

| Prediction Outcome | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Exact Scoreline** | You predicted the exact scores for both teams (e.g., predicted `2-1` and match ended `2-1`). |
| **Correct Result** | You predicted the correct winner or draw, but not the exact scoreline (e.g., predicted `3-1`, match ended `2-0`). |
| **Wrong Prediction** | The prediction did not match the winner, draw, or scoreline. |

---

## ⚡ Bonus Points

In addition to standard match prediction points, you can earn extra bonus points:

### 1. Streak Bonus
* **Consecutive Predictions**: If you predict the correct result/scoreline consecutively, you trigger a streak.
* **Streak Threshold**: Once your streak matches or exceeds the threshold set by the admin (e.g., 3 consecutive correct results), you will earn additional bonus points per consecutive correct match.

### 2. Leaderboard Winners Bonuses
* **Daily Winner Bonus**: Awarded to the player(s) with the highest score on any single calendar day.
* **Weekly Winner Bonus**: Awarded to the player(s) with the highest score over a match week.
* **Monthly Winner Bonus**: Awarded to the player(s) with the highest score over a month.

> [!TIP]
> Keep an eye on the **How Points Work** banner on the Home page and the website Footer. These values are updated dynamically in real-time if the administrator adjusts the point configurations!
